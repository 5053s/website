About Us

Who we are

We're a professional community for latinxs in tech. A space where latinxs can network and find better opportunities for their career, celebrating the diversity in the community, bring access to tech for low income latinx communities and increase the opportunities for latinxs in tech.

What we do

We maintain online communities where we promote conversations around different topics that are valuable for the community; like career advice, work opportunities, events and information relevant to our members.

We host events for our members in our different chapters, where they can meet, network, socialize and create stronger bonds.

We partner with other organizations that work in the diversity space, or for the latinx community; to provide help in different ways we can, like attending their events, volunteering, spreading the word about their communities.
